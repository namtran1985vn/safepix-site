# Privacy Policy for PixelShield

**Last updated: March 2026**

PixelShield respects your privacy. This app is designed to enhance photos using on-device AI while keeping your data private.

## On-Device Photo Processing

All photo enhancement is performed locally on your device using Apple's Core ML technology.

Your photos never leave your device. PixelShield does not upload, store, or transmit photos to any server.

## Data Collection

PixelShield does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app does NOT collect:

- Photos
- Personal information
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Location data

## Third-Party Services

PixelShield does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

## Internet Usage

PixelShield can function completely offline. An internet connection is not required for photo enhancement.

## Children's Privacy

PixelShield does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
