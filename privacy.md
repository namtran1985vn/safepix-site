# Privacy Policy for SafePix

SafePix respects your privacy.

## Photo Processing

**All photo processing happens locally on the user's device. No photos are uploaded or stored externally.**

SafePix processes photos entirely on your device using Apple's Core ML technology.

Photos are never uploaded to external servers.

## Data Collection

SafePix does not collect personal data.

We do not collect:
- Photos
- Personal information
- Device identifiers

## Internet Usage

SafePix can work completely offline.
