# SafePix Support

SafePix is a private AI photo enhancer that works entirely on your device.

## More about SafePix

**Bring blurry, low-quality photos back to life** with powerful AI that runs on your device. SafePix restores details, upscales images, and sharpens faces without uploading anything to the cloud. Your photos stay private—always.

- **Restore blurry photos** — Turn soft or low-quality images into clear, detailed photos while keeping them natural.
- **AI upscale** — Increase resolution without losing quality. Great for old photos, cropped or zoomed images, and small pictures from messaging apps.
- **Improve faces and portraits** — Enhance facial details and clarity while keeping skin and features looking natural.
- **Works offline** — Uses Apple’s Core ML on your device. No internet needed. Use it on planes, while traveling, or anywhere.
- **Private by design** — No cloud processing, no data collection, no uploads. Your photos never leave your device.
- **Simple and fast** — Choose a photo, tap Enhance, save. Enhancements in seconds.

Perfect for restoring old photos, fixing blurry images, improving portraits, and upscaling before sharing.
