SafePix is a private AI photo enhancer that works entirely on your device.

## FAQ

### Do you upload my photos?
No. SafePix processes photos locally on your device.

### Does SafePix require internet?
No. SafePix works completely offline.

### What does SafePix do?
SafePix enhances blurry photos, restores details, and upscales images using on-device AI.

### Do you collect user data?
No. SafePix does not collect or store personal data.
